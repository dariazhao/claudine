Designed with warmth

Emotional vocabulary, not emoji.

Most wellness apps reduce feelings to a five-point scale or a color. Claudine's check-in uses sixteen distinct moods — drawn from Plutchik's Wheel of Emotions — each one a bear face designed to be recognized, not decoded.

The bear isn't a mascot. It's a design decision: familiarity lowers the barrier to honest reflection. Your 78-year-old grandmother will tap "worried" more readily than she'll write "I'm anxious today."
